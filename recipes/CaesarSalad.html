Classic Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • Salad:
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Dressing:
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional Add-ons:
  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Bacon bits
  • Anchovies

Steps

Step 1: Prepare the dressing by whisking together the mayonnaise, garlic, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 2: In a large bowl, toss the chopped romaine lettuce with the dressing until evenly coated.

Step 3: Top the salad with croutons, Parmesan cheese, and optional add-ons like grilled chicken or bacon bits.

Step 4: Serve immediately and enjoy your Caesar Salad!

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 300 kcal
  • Protein: 8 g
  • Fat: 25 g
  • Carbohydrates: 10 g
  • Fiber: 3 g
  • Sugar: 2 g
